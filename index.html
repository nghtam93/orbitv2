THE NEW PARADIGM

CULTURAL PRESERVATION

The series of game ecosystems that recreate 7 civilizations that are considered prosperous and set a milestone for the development of all humanity.

UNLIMITED ADVENTURE

A compelling game story with closely linked storyline that can expand to many other games. The adventures that contain a lot of surprises and attractions.

VARIETY OF GAMEPLAY

Not only stopping at the basic modes, when new games are released, there will be more special game modes.

CREATOR SPACE

The platform allows participants to create NFT products and gain ownership.

SOCIALFI WITH 360 LIVESTREAM FEATURE

A digital society where the community can interact, participate in activities like real life and make money from them.

INTEGRATION OF VR/AR

The integration of virtual reality technology devices is to bring the most realistic experience for the players when participating in the metaverse from a first-person perspective.

INTEGRATING DEFI PLATFORMS

The perfect combination of GameFi and DeFi offers a more diverse experience but still ensures profits.
